🥬 素 Vegetarian 與 齋 Fasting 國小版（純中文＋英文hover）

素 Vegetarian
定義 Definition

素食主義是不吃肉，吃植物、蛋和奶的飲食方式。

為什麼 Why

有些人選擇素食，是因為想保護動物，還有保持身體健康。

舉例 Example

就像穿舒服的衣服，不穿真皮草，因為你愛動物。

Vegetarian
Definition

Vegetarianism means not eating meat but eating plants, eggs, and milk.

Why

Some people choose vegetarian food because they want to protect animals and stay healthy.

Example

It is like wearing comfortable clothes and not wearing real fur because you love animals.

齋 Fasting
定義 Definition

齋戒是不吃肉，也不吃蔥、蒜這些辛辣的食物。

為什麼 Why

這樣做可以讓身心保持清淨，讓心情平靜。

舉例 Example

就像考試前保持安靜，專心準備。

Fasting
Definition

Fasting means not eating meat and avoiding spicy foods like onions and garlic.

Why

It helps keep the body and mind pure and calm.

Example

It is like staying quiet and focused before a test.
