素食與齋戒與永續發展目標（SDG）雙語學習 - 高中版

素 Vegetarian

素食主義是避免食用肉類、魚類和家禽，通常會攝取蛋與乳製品。這種飲食反映對生命的尊重，並有助於減少環境負擔。

選擇素食與聯合國永續發展目標中關於負責任消費和氣候行動的理念相符，鼓勵我們減少浪費並保護地球。

Vegetarian

Vegetarianism avoids eating meat, fish, and poultry, and usually includes eggs and dairy. This diet reflects respect for life and helps reduce environmental impact.

Choosing vegetarian diets aligns with the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals on responsible consumption and climate action, encouraging waste reduction and protecting the planet.

齋 Fasting

齋戒是一種更嚴格的飲食習慣，不僅不食用肉類，也避免蔥蒜等辛辣食物，目的是淨化身心，提升精神專注。

此習慣呼應永續發展目標中關於健康與福祉的精神，強調身體健康與內在平衡。

Fasting

Fasting is a stricter diet that avoids meat and pungent foods like onions and garlic. Its purpose is to purify the body and mind and enhance mental focus.

This practice resonates with the Sustainable Development Goal on health and well-being, emphasizing physical health and inner balance.

永續發展目標與日常生活

聯合國永續發展目標（SDGs）指導全球行動，促進環境保護、社會公平與經濟發展。素食和齋戒是我們落實這些目標的有效方式，幫助減少碳排放並提升身心健康。

  • 列舉兩個你能在日常生活中實踐永續發展的行動。
  • 討論：為何尊重生命和環境對社會很重要？

Sustainable Development Goals and Daily Life

The United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) guide global actions to promote environmental protection, social equity, and economic growth. Vegetarianism and fasting are effective ways to achieve these goals, helping reduce carbon emissions and improve well-being.

Suggested Learning Activities

  • Identify three unfamiliar English words in the text, write their meanings in Chinese, and create sentences using them.
  • Explain in your own words how vegetarianism and fasting support the Sustainable Development Goals.
  • List two actions you can take in your daily life to practice sustainability.
  • Discuss: Why is respect for life and the environment important to society?
