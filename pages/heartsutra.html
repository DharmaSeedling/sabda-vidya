📜 般若波羅蜜多心經

大唐三藏法師 玄奘 譯｜James Legge, 1815–1897 英譯

🙏 開經偈

無上甚深微妙法，百千萬劫難遭遇。
我今見聞得受持，願解如來真實義。

Homage to the Supreme Dharma: profound and subtle, rare to encounter through eons. Now that I see, hear, and receive it, may I understand the Tathāgata’s true intent.

📖 經文對照

觀自在 菩薩， 行 深 般若波羅蜜多時， 照見 五蘊皆空， 度 一切 苦厄。
(Legge) Avalokiteshvara, while practicing deeply the Prajnaparamita, clearly saw that the five aggregates are all empty, and so relieved all suffering.
(Plain English B1) When Avalokiteshvara was deeply meditating, he saw that everything is empty, and this helped him overcome suffering.
