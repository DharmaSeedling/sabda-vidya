🍽️ Cultural Differences | 文化差異比較

🇬🇧 United Kingdom

  • Vegetarian-friendly: Very accommodating with clearly marked menus
  • Pub culture: Most pubs now offer vegetarian options
  • Supermarkets: Extensive vegetarian and vegan sections
  • Common cuisines: Indian, Middle Eastern, Mediterranean

英國對素食者非常友善，菜單標示清楚，連傳統酒吧都有素食選擇。

🇫🇷 France

  • Traditional approach: More focus on fresh vegetables and cheese
  • Regional variations: Mediterranean south more vegetarian-friendly
  • Restaurants: Ask specifically, as vegetarian isn't always obvious
  • Market culture: Excellent fresh produce in local markets

法國傳統上重視新鮮蔬菜和乳製品，南部地中海地區對素食者更友善。
