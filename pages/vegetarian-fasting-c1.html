素 Vegetarian
定義 Definition

素食主義（Vegetarianism）是一種飲食習慣，主張不食用肉類、魚類及家禽，並通常包含蛋類與乳製品。

原因 Why

選擇素食通常基於道德考量、健康需求與環境永續等多重原因，展現對生命尊重與自然保護的承諾。

舉例 Example

就像選擇穿著環保材質的衣物，既保護動物權益，也支持永續時尚。

Vegetarian
Definition

Vegetarianism is a dietary practice that excludes meat, fish, and poultry, and often includes eggs and dairy products.

Why

Choosing vegetarianism is often motivated by ethical considerations, health concerns, and environmental sustainability, reflecting a commitment to respect for life and nature conservation.

Example

It is like choosing to wear eco-friendly clothing, which protects animal rights and supports sustainable fashion.

齋 Fasting
定義 Definition

齋戒是一種嚴格的飲食規範，除了不吃肉外，也避免食用蔥、蒜等辛辣刺激的食物。

原因 Why

齋戒的目的是為了淨化身心，提升精神專注力與修行品質。

舉例 Example

就像在重要考試前保持安靜與專注，為心靈準備最好的狀態。

Fasting
Definition

Fasting is a strict dietary discipline that excludes meat and also pungent foods such as onions and garlic.

Why

The purpose of fasting is to purify body and mind, enhancing mental focus and the quality of spiritual practice.

Example

It is like maintaining silence and focus before an important examination to prepare the mind.
