📜 般若波羅蜜多心經

中文譯本：大唐三藏法師 玄奘（602–664）

🙏 開經偈

無上 甚深 微妙法， 百千萬劫 難遭遇。
我今見聞得受持，願解如來真實義。

Homage to the Supreme Dharma: profound and subtle, rare to encounter through eons.
Now that I see, hear, and receive it, may I understand the Tathāgata’s true intent.

📖 經文對照

觀自在 菩薩， 行 深 般若波羅蜜多時， 照見 五蘊皆空， 度 一切 苦厄。

Legge: Avalokiteshvara, while practicing deeply the Prajnaparamita, clearly saw that the five aggregates are all empty, and so relieved all suffering.

B1: Avalokiteshvara saw that everything is empty, and it helped him overcome suffering.

舍利子， 色 不異 空， 空不異色； 色即是空， 空即是色； 受想行識， 亦復如是。

Legge: Form is not different from emptiness, and emptiness is not different from form. Form is emptiness; emptiness is form. So too are feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness.

B1: Form is the same as emptiness. Feeling and thought are the same too.
