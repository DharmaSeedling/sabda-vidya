📜 般若波羅蜜多心經

🕉️ Heart Sutra

🙏 開經偈

無上甚深微妙法， 百千萬劫難遭遇。
我今見聞得受持， 願解如來真實義。

Legge: Homage to the Supreme Dharma: profound and subtle, rare to encounter through eons. Now that I see, hear, and receive it, may I understand the Tathāgata’s true intent.
B1: We honor the great teaching, deep and special, hard to find for ages. Now I see and hear it, and I hope to understand its true meaning.

📖 經文對照

觀自在菩薩， 行深般若波羅蜜多時， 照見， 五蘊皆空， 度一切苦厄。

Avalokiteshvara, while practicing deeply the Prajnaparamita, clearly saw that the five aggregates are all empty, and so relieved all suffering.

Avalokiteshvara saw that everything is empty, and it helped him overcome suffering.

舍利子， 色不異空， 空不異色； 色即是空， 空即是色； 受想行識， 亦復如是。

Legge: Shariputra, form is not different from emptiness, and emptiness is not different from form. Form is emptiness; emptiness is form. So too are feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness.
B1: Shariputra, form is the same as emptiness. Feeling, thought, action, and awareness are the same too.

舍利子， 是諸法空相， 不生不滅， 不垢不淨， 不增不減。

Legge: Shariputra, all phenomena have the characteristics of emptiness: they are not born, they do not die, they are not defiled, they are not pure, they do not increase, they do not decrease.
B1: Shariputra, everything is empty. It doesn’t start, end, get dirty, get clean, grow, or shrink.

是故空中 無色， 無受想行識； 無眼耳鼻舌身意， 無色聲香味觸法。

Legge: Therefore, in emptiness, there is no form, no sensation, perception, formation, or consciousness; no eyes, ears, nose, tongue, body, or mind; no forms, sounds, smells, tastes, tactile objects, or dharmas.
B1: So, in emptiness, there is no form, feeling, thought, action, or awareness; no eyes, ears, nose, tongue, body, or mind; no sights, sounds, smells, tastes, touches, or ideas.

無眼界， 乃至無意識界； 無無明， 亦無無明盡， 乃至無老死， 亦無老死盡。

Legge: No eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, mind; no form, sound, smell, taste, touch, or dharmas; no realm of eyes and so forth until no realm of mind consciousness; no ignorance and also no extinction of ignorance; and so forth until no old age and death and also no extinction of old age and death.
B1: There is no eye realm, up to no mind consciousness realm; no ignorance, no end of ignorance; up to no old age and death, no end of old age and death.

無苦集滅道， 無智， 亦無得， 以無所得故。

Legge: There is no suffering, no origin, no cessation, no path. There is no wisdom and no gain. Because nothing is gained.
B1: No suffering, no cause, no end, no path. No wisdom or gains. Nothing to get.

菩提薩埵， 依 般若波羅蜜多故， 心無罣礙； 無罣礙故， 無有恐怖。

Legge: Because bodhisattvas rely on Prajnaparamita, their minds have no hindrance; because there is no hindrance, they have no fear.
B1: Bodhisattvas rely on wisdom, so their minds have no obstacles and no fear.

遠離 顛倒夢想， 究竟 涅槃。

Legge: They are far from delusions and reach Nirvana.
B1: They leave illusions behind and reach Nirvana.

三世諸佛， 依般若波羅蜜多故， 得阿耨多羅三藐三菩提。

Legge: The Buddhas of the three times rely on Prajnaparamita and attain Anuttara Samyak Sambodhi.
B1: Buddhas of past, present, and future rely on wisdom to become fully enlightened.

故知般若波羅蜜多 是大神咒， 是大明咒， 是無上咒， 是無等等咒。

Legge: Therefore know that Prajnaparamita is the great mantra, the bright mantra, the supreme mantra, the unequalled mantra.
B1: So know that this wisdom is the great mantra, the bright mantra, the best mantra, the only mantra.

能除一切苦， 真實不虛。

Legge: Which removes all suffering, true and not false.
B1: It removes all pain, it is true.

故說般若波羅蜜多咒， 即說咒曰：

Legge: Thus is the Prajnaparamita mantra proclaimed.
B1: So we say the wisdom mantra.

揭諦 揭諦 波羅揭諦 波羅僧揭諦 菩提薩婆訶。

Legge: Gate gate paragate parasamgate bodhi svaha.
B1: Go! Go! Go beyond! Go fully beyond! Enlightenment! Hurray!
