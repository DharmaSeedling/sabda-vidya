Vegetarian
Definition

Vegetarianism means not eating meat but eating plants, eggs, and milk.

Why

Some people choose vegetarian food because they want to protect animals and stay healthy.

Example

It is like wearing comfortable clothes and not wearing real fur because you love animals.

Definition

Why
